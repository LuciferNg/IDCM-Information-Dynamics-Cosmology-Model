**Scope of responsibilities:**

- The calibrator is **not** a moral arbiter — $\varepsilon \cdot \beta > 0$ is a structural condition, not a standard of good and evil
- The calibrator is **not** the sole interpreter of the theory — the recursion operates without needing anyone to understand it
- The calibrator is **not** a ruler of society — adjusting boundary conditions is not equivalent to controlling content
