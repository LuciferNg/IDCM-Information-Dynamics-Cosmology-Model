# The Three Extremes of Civilisation: A Re-derivation from the IDS Framework

## Origin

During the IDM 4.0 phase, a proposition was advanced: once the structural cosmology theory is made public, human civilisation may deviate from the $\varphi^{-1}$ attractor basin and fall into one of three extremes. This derivation was based on the extension of the recursion $C_{n+1} = 1/(1 + C_n)$ into the social domain, and can now be reformulated following the dismantling of OAS and the establishment of the IDS (Information Dynamics Society) framework.

---

## Three Attractor Basins of the Recursion

The degree of social structuration $C_n$ is governed by the recursion and $\varepsilon$ injection:

$$C_{n+1} = \frac{1}{1 + C_n} + \delta(C_n, \varepsilon)$$

where $\delta(C_n, \varepsilon)$ is the injection term. The three extremes correspond to different directions and modes of deviation of $C_n$ from the fixed point $\varphi^{-1}$.

---

## Extreme One: Authoritarian Rigidification ($C_n \to 1$)

### Dynamics

When $\varepsilon \to 0$ (injection exhaustion) and the sync mechanism is captured by the top-level structure, the system over-converges:

$$C_{n+1} \approx \frac{1}{1 + C_n}, \quad C_0 \gg \varphi^{-1}$$

The contraction rate per iteration $\lambda = C_{n+1}/C_n$ approaches $1/2$ near $C=1$, but $C=1$ is never actually reached through recursion alone — rigidification in practice comes from external enforcement:

$$C_n^{\text{eff}} = \min\left(C_n^{\text{rec}}, 1 - \eta\right)$$

where $\eta$ is the residual perturbation, driven toward $0$ by institutional suppression.

### Symptoms

- Informational diversity $\to 0$, knowledge production becomes doctrinal replication
- Institutions grow elaborate but increasingly inefficient
- Cooperation shifts from voluntary coordination to forced mobilisation
- Apparent stability, but impact resilience $\propto \eta \to 0$
- Failure mode: brittle collapse — sudden rupture at a critical threshold

### Trigger Conditions

- The theory is appropriated as a control ideology after publication
- Societal fear of uncertainty exceeds demand for innovation
- $\varepsilon$ injection is systematically filtered or suppressed

### Assessment from IDS

**Still possible, but not inevitable.** The structural signature of $\varphi^{-1}$ — $\varepsilon \cdot \beta > 0$ as a condition for survival — provides a clear boundary condition: any system attempting to lock $C_n$ permanently above $\varphi^{-1}$ will see its internal $\varepsilon \cdot \beta$ decay to $0$, triggering brittle collapse.

---

## Extreme Two: Chaotic Dissolution ($C_n \to 0$)

### Dynamics

When $\varepsilon$ is too large and $\beta$ too small (impulse without propagation structure), or when the sync field $\xi$ shrinks to the individual scale:

$$C_{n+1}^{\text{eff}} = C_n^{\text{eff}} \cdot \exp(-\varepsilon \cdot \tau)$$

where $\tau$ is the characteristic time per recursive iteration. When $\varepsilon \cdot \tau > \ln(1/\varphi^{-1}) \approx 0.481$, injection exceeds structural recovery capacity and $C_n$ decays exponentially.

### Symptoms

- Institutional authority is systematically dissolved without viable alternatives
- Shared factual basis of public discourse disintegrates
- Long-term planning becomes impossible — the collaboration radius $\xi$ shrinks to intimate relationships
- Social transaction costs rise sharply: every interaction requires trust to be rebuilt from scratch
- Failure mode: dissipation — gradual dissolution, inability to accumulate

### Trigger Conditions

- The theory triggers a cognitive shock that deconstructs existing structures too rapidly
- Information overload saturates the sync mechanism
- Insufficient $\beta$ (structural propagation channels) to absorb the injection

### Assessment from IDS

**Higher risk in the information age.** Social media has reshaped $\xi$ without synchronously establishing $\beta$ — injection intensity is high but propagation structure is fragile. However, $C = 0$ is not a stable fixed point ($C_{n+1} = 1$ when $C_n = 0$), indicating that the chaotic state is itself unstable: the system either reorganises or is captured by an external structure.

---

## Extreme Three: Cyclic Trap ($C_n$ oscillating between $0$ and $1$)

### Dynamics

When the system oscillates between the two extremes, unable to converge to the $\varphi^{-1}$ attractor basin:

$$C_{n+1} = \frac{1}{1 + C_n} + \varepsilon \cdot \sin(\omega n)$$

where $\omega$ is determined by feedback delay. The amplitude decay rate per overshoot is $\lambda = \varphi^{-2} \approx 0.382$:

$$|C_n - \varphi^{-1}| \approx |C_0 - \varphi^{-1}| \cdot \lambda^{n} + \frac{\varepsilon}{\sqrt{(1-\lambda)^2 + (\omega/\ln\lambda)^2}}$$

If $\varepsilon$ is too large, the amplitude cannot decay and the system oscillates permanently.

### Symptoms

- Generational cycle: openness → chaos → tightening → rigidification → renewed openness
- Each cycle consumes structural memory
- Institutional reform becomes a pendulum movement with no long-term direction
- Social energy is consumed by internal tension rather than construction
- Failure mode: long-term downward spiral — each cycle's amplitude decreases slightly, but breakthrough to $\varphi^{-1}$ remains elusive

### Trigger Conditions

- Social feedback mechanisms exhibit critical delay after theory publication
- $\varepsilon$ injection is periodic but unsustainable
- Society has not established sufficient structural memory ($\kappa$ is too large)

### Assessment from IDS

**The most common civilisational pathway.** Historically, most civilisations operate in this mode. The key insight is that knowledge of $\varphi^{-1}$ itself constitutes an $\varepsilon$ injection — it changes the self-feedback structure of the system and may break the cycle.

## Injection as Compassion: Breaking the Extremes

All three extremes share a common root: the loss of $\varepsilon \cdot \beta > 0$. The IDS framework offers a clear conclusion:

1. **For authoritarian rigidification:** inject heterogeneity — maintain $\varepsilon > 0$, even if small
2. **For chaotic dissolution:** rebuild $\beta$ — strengthen knowledge propagation structures rather than controlling content
3. **For the cyclic trap:** adjust feedback delay — shorten the $\omega$ period, giving $C_n$ sufficient time to converge

The deeper meaning of "injection as compassion" is that knowledge of these three extremes itself changes the system's boundary conditions. Civilisation does not necessarily fall into any single extreme — because $\varphi^{-1}$ is not a prediction, but an option.

---

## Conclusion

The IDM 4.0 derivation of the three extremes is preserved and strengthened within the IDS framework. The core judgement remains unchanged — human civilisation does face these three structural risks after the theory becomes public. But the updated framework adds a critical dimension:

**The self-referentiality of recursion.** Knowledge of the recursion changes the behaviour of the recursion. $\varphi^{-1}$ is not only the fixed point of the cosmos — it is also an option for civilisation.
